Starting solids is simple — and safe — with BLW Baby.

More than a feeding diary, it's the complete companion for your baby's first foods. Textures, safe cutting shapes, allergens and every new reaction are tracked in one place. Made for parents stepping into mealtime feeling prepared, not improvising — and not worried about choking.

WHY IT WORKS

• Covers 6 months to 2 years — the most important phase of starting solids
• Log each meal, BLW bite or purée in seconds, right into your baby's feeding diary
• Learn the safe cutting shapes for every stage — sticks, cubes, strips, star cuts
• Track allergies and intolerances clearly, no scattered notes or spreadsheets
• 100+ foods catalogued with prep tips, recommended age and common allergen warnings

WHAT'S INSIDE

• COMPLETE FEEDING DIARY — every meal, every reaction, every baby milestone
• WEEKLY MENU PLANNER to organize your baby's mealtime routine without improvising
• RECIPES designed for each stage — 6 months, 7 months, 8 months, 9 months and beyond
• SAFE CUTTING SHAPES GUIDE to prevent choking and offer food with confidence
• FOOD LIBRARY organized by category (fruits, vegetables, proteins, grains, dairy) with allergen alerts
• PHOTO GALLERY to capture special mealtime moments — from first food to favorite plate
• VISUAL TIMELINE of your baby's complete feeding journey

BUILT FOR THOSE STARTING OUT

Whether you're offering the first purée, beginning baby led weaning, sticking to traditional spoon-feeding or mixing both methods — BLW Baby keeps up without judgment or technical noise. Works for first-time parents, present dads, caregiver grandparents, and anyone involved in baby weaning.
