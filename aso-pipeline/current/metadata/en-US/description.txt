Track your baby's food introduction journey simply, organized, and safely with BLW Baby!

BLW Baby is the complete app for parents who want to record and track their baby's entire food journey, from first foods to a varied and healthy diet.

KEY FEATURES:

• COMPLETE FOOD DIARY
Record every food offered to your baby, including date, acceptance level, and notes. Track progress and see which foods have been tried.

• FOOD LIBRARY
Over 100 foods organized by category (fruits, vegetables, proteins, grains, and dairy). Each food includes information about recommended age, preparation methods, and potential allergens.

• PHOTO GALLERY
Capture and organize photos of special mealtime moments. Create visual memories of your baby's food journey.

• PRACTICAL RECIPES
Discover simple and nutritious recipes for each stage of food introduction. Creative ideas to offer new flavors.

• ALLERGEN TRACKING
Identify and track major allergens. Record reactions and maintain a complete history to share with your pediatrician.

• BLW TIPS
Learn about the Baby-Led Weaning method with practical safety tips, appropriate cuts, and how to make mealtimes more enjoyable.

WHY CHOOSE BLW BABY?

 Intuitive and easy-to-use interface
 Works offline - your data stays saved on device
 No annoying ads
 Based on recommendations from child nutrition specialists

WHO IS THIS APP FOR?

• Parents starting food introduction (from 6 months)
• Families following the BLW (Baby-Led Weaning) method
• Parents who prefer traditional food introduction
• Caregivers and grandparents involved in baby feeding
